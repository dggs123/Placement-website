The Career Guidance Cell facilitates the students to explore the world of employment and new career avenues. It also helps students in career planning through counseling where in they gather information about themselves in terms of their interests, aptitudes and other abilities so that they can choose their career more effectively. The objective of the Cell is also on the professional development of the students by training them towards employability skills with personality development training ,motivational talks, counseling , etc.. which in turn helps the students to move into a desired occupation and apply in prestigious postgraduate and professional schools.

The Career Guidance Cell also helps the students to prepare with GRE, GATE, CAT and other competitive examinations. Placement

Process

The placement process for the Faculty of Engineering has been formulated in line with the policy of the University, to efficiently streamline the recruitment experience to both the students and the recruiter.

1. Registration of Students

The students willing to participate in the Campus Recruitment Process would be required to register their consent with the CGC ( Career Guidance Cell ) through the Knowledge-Pro.

2. Pre- placement Talks

The Pre-placement talks shall be conducted , if asked by the recruiter , prior to the beginning of the Pre Interview screening .This is to facilitates interaction between the students and the potential recruiters.

3. Pre Interview Screening

  • The procedure for pre Interview screening ( if any )is recruiters own policy.
  • The applicants selected in the pre interviews screening shall qualify for appearing in the final Interviews.

    • 4. The Final Interview

    List shall be made as per the recruiters recruitment policy, or according to the results of the Pre-Interview Screening conducted by the Recruiter.

    5. Final Offer

    Based on the interview results, the recruiter decides to give an offer to the student to join the organization and would expect to give consent within a stipulated period of time.

    6. Miscellaneous

    (a) Registered students are expected to refer to the CGC notice board on the Ground floor of Block II, Faculty of Engineering. Registered students are also expected to be in touch with their respective department Faculty-in Charge / Placement coordinator / Student Placement representative on a regular basis regarding updates on the placement activity.
    (b) Students should be well groomed and should be dressed in the institute formal suit.
    (c) Students should carry at least 2 copies of their CVs and Mark cards in a folder along with 3 passport size photographs and ID card for the placement process.
